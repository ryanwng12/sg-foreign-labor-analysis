What Does This Tell Us?

Online sentiment reveals deep-seated anxiety about job competition and cultural change — but also genuine empathy for foreign workers. The conversation is more nuanced than headlines suggest.

Data collection: 4,000+ posts scraped from Reddit (r/singapore, r/askSingapore, r/SingaporeRaw) and The Independent SG (2015–2025). Reddit accounts for ~93% of posts; The Independent SG contributes ~7%. When filtering by source, sample sizes vary significantly.
Classification: Each post was classified by OpenAI GPT-4o-mini for stance (concerned / supportive / neutral), topic, and key themes. This is automated NLP classification, not human annotation — some misclassification is expected.
Limitations: Online forums skew younger, English-speaking, and more opinionated. This is not a representative survey of Singaporean public opinion.
